# Privacy Policy for Oliver the Content Poster

_Last Updated: 2025_

Oliver the Content Poster ("Oliver") is a private automation tool created 
and used solely by me. This Privacy Policy describes how data is handled within 
the tool.

## 1. Information Collected
Oliver does not collect or store personal information from any users other than 
myself. Only the files I intentionally upload or process (e.g., videos) are 
temporarily handled during automation.

## 2. How Information Is Used
Information processed by Oliver is used exclusively for:
- Uploading videos to my own TikTok account
- Scheduling and managing personal content
- Triggering workflows through n8n and Telegram

## 3. Data Sharing
Oliver does not share, sell, or distribute any data to third parties.

## 4. Data Storage
No long-term storage of any personal data occurs. Data is processed only as 
needed for posting content.

## 5. Third-Party Services
Oliver may interact with services including:
- TikTok APIs
- Telegram
- n8n automation workflows

Each service follows its own privacy policies.
